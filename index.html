Founded in Leyton area of London back in 1975 by bassist Steve Harris—the only original member still playing with the band today—Iron Maiden have become one of the biggest bands in the world. Since the release of debut album Iron Maiden in 1980, the British heavyweights have released 16 full-length studio albums, and sold over 100 million copies.
